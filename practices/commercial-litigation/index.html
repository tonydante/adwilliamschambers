Commercial Litigation

The litigation practice of Adwilliams chambers focuses on managing corporate risks and furthering the client’s strategic business objectives. With these key principles in mind, we work on solving the litigation problem in the most advantageous and efficient manner. Our unwillingness to let litigation “take on a life of its own” separates us from our competitors. Our litigators are aggressive, creative, and relentless in accomplishing their task. The attorneys of Adwilliams chambers appear in litigation and arbitration forums throughout the United States and Europe. The litigation practice encompasses the range of matters faced by our corporate and entrepreneurial clients:

  • pre-litigation counseling and risk assessments
  • contract disputes
  • director and officer fiduciary duty counseling
  • business torts, including breach of fiduciary duty, fraud, conspiracy, aiding and abetting liability, and misappropriation of confidential and trade secret information
  • internal investigations, including financial accounting and reporting, fraud, and Foreign Corrupt Practices Act violations
  • investor fraud claims, including Ponzi schemes and receivership proceedings
  • real estate
  • international disputes
  • partnership disputes
  • securities litigation
  • regulatory matters, including FINRA, SEC, and Justice Department inquires
  • trademark and copyright infringement
  • breach of warranty
  • lender liability
  • employment matters including FINRA, SEC, and Justice Department inquires
  • trademark and copyright infringement
  • employment matters
