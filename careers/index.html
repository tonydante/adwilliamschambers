Careers

At the core of E/N’s culture is the belief that large, traditional law firms are no longer essential for the practice of top-tier law; certainly not for representing non-billion-dollar clients. We believe there is a better way to attract, retain, and support the market’s most talented lawyers, and it includes combining top compensation, flexible work arrangements with a heavy prioritization of work-life balance, and a focused, entrepreneurial culture dedicated to efficiency and client satisfaction.

The ideal E/N candidate will include the following:

  • Exceptional credentials;
  • Proven business development and client management skills;
  • A passion for leveraging technology and process in the practice of law;
  • An interest in an entrepreneurial, self-driven work environment, including the possibility of working remotely;
  • A belief in maintaining a respectful, collaborative firm culture;
  • Experience in one of E/N’s core practice areas; and
  • A desire to build a top-tier law practice under a new law firm model.

If this sounds like you, please contact us at careers@adwilliams.com with a resume or LinkedIn profile link.  Qualified candidates outside of existing E/N markets are encouraged to apply.

For law students and junior attorneys, see information regarding E/N’s Tech & VC Fellows Program.

Exceptionally talented non-attorneys are encouraged to apply to E/N’s Advanced Paralegal Program.
